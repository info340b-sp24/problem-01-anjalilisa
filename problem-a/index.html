Katniss Everdeen

Katniss Everdeen is the main character in The Hunger Games. She is from District 12 which is a poor district. Katniss ends up being selected for the Hunger Games, a competition where district children fight to the death. This eventually sparks a rebellion that dismantles the entire structure of their government.

Katniss is cool because:

  • She provided for her family from a young age.
  • She is great at using a bow and arrow
  • She volunteered for the hunger games competition to save her sister.
